Learnloom Teacher's Code of Conduct

The following guidelines are designed to ensure a professional and respectful learning environment for both our teachers and students. Adherence to these standards is essential for maintaining the integrity and quality of the Learnloom platform.

  • Punctuality

    All teachers are required to join their scheduled online sessions at least 2 minutes prior to the start time. This allows for a smooth and timely start to the class.

  • Professionalism

    Teachers must maintain a professional demeanor at all times. This includes dressing in a professional manner suitable for an educational setting.

  • Classroom Etiquette

    To maintain focus and a respectful environment, eating is strictly prohibited during live tutoring sessions.

  • Platform Integrity

    The Learnloom platform and its resources are to be used for educational purposes only. Misuse of the platform is a violation of this code of conduct.

  • Distraction-Free Environment

    To ensure students can focus, please mute any TV, radio, or other background music during sessions.

  • Adherence to Learning Style

    We ask that you accompany the established Learnloom learning style and avoid incorporating a personal style that deviates from our methodology.

  • Game and Tool Vetting

    All external games and tools must be vetted and approved before being used in a live class. Do not use games that contain unwanted or inappropriate advertisements.

  • Privacy and Recording Policy

    No video or audio recording of sessions is permitted by teachers. Any recording found will result in the immediate termination of the contract.

  • Background Checks

    For compliance with international regulations and to ensure child safety, a police certificate or equivalent background check is required for teachers working with clients in certain regions.

  • Attendance and No-Shows

    If a student is late, please use the support channel to notify us so we can follow up. In the case of a no-show, inform us immediately and mark the attendance in the system. Accurate attendance is crucial for client billing.

  • Leave and Payroll

    For emergency leave, please notify us in advance. Payroll is generated at the end of each month; for any payroll-related issues, please reach out to your coordinator.

  • Profile and Onboarding

    Please upload a professional headshot (e.g., in a blazer) to your profile. After your probationary period, remember to update your profile with your complete address in the portal.
